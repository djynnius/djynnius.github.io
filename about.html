Learning

I teach datascience with python and R. While you do not need to know much to get into the trainig it may help to know enough math to carry out basic operations such as as addition, subtraction, multiplication and division (arithmetic), and what it means to say \(x = 3\) or \(y = 4.5\) (algebra). Having some excel skill can be helpful too but is not required.
