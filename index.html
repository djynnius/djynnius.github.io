Course Details

This course is designed to provide you with the introduction to proficient level training in data science. It is designed for people who want to take on data science as a career as well as for those who want to explore the opportunities that exist in the field. There are a number of tools for data science, this course focuses on python3 as the major tool/programing language.

No prior knowledge of programming or data science is required. Join us in this conversational approach of moving from zero to proficiency. It will be worth your time! The course is designed to be generic allowing you as a participant to apply what you learn to your industry/profession. We will cover the required statistics, data cleaning, visualization, hypothesis testing and gets as far as machine learning and deep learning.

Everyone gets to benefit from the course including people who have no prior experience and people who want to learn and have previously tried some other course and it didn't work out. Here's your second chance at understanding the concepts and becoming proficient!
